Our Story

A Name Built on Family

ARMAN is more than a place to stay — it is a reflection of family, care, and thoughtful hospitality. The name itself holds deep meaning, formed from the initials of our family: Asif, Rehana, Mubeen, Adnaan, and Naailah.

What began as a personal vision has evolved into a boutique-style experience, where every detail is considered with our guests in mind. Located at 30 B RG Mugabe Way in Newtown, Kwekwe, we invite you to experience the warmth and care that defines ARMAN Lodge.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about staying at ARMAN Lodge, our amenities, house rules, and how to book.

Every suite at ARMAN Lodge includes free WiFi, Netflix, DStv Compact on a smart TV, a gas geyser for hot water, backup power, a portable air cooler, and complimentary tea and coffee facilities. Daily housekeeping and an on-site night guard are also included at no extra charge.
ARMAN Lodge is located at 30 B RG Mugabe Way, Newtown, Kwekwe, Zimbabwe. Newtown is a peaceful and well-established residential suburb, conveniently close to Kwekwe's central business district, supermarkets, and local dining.
Simply contact us directly to check availability and book your suite. You can reach us via WhatsApp at +263 786 138 798. You can also use the Booking Assistant on this site to send us your enquiry.
Our short-term suites are available for a minimum of one night. There is no maximum stay limit — contact us for extended stay rates. Please note that check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 10:00 AM.
We kindly ask guests to observe the following: no smoking indoors, the property is not pet friendly, and we prefer no alcohol and no pork on the premises. These guidelines help us maintain a respectful and comfortable environment for all guests.
Each short-term suite is a fully self-catered 2-bedroom apartment consisting of 1 double bed and 2 single beds (sleeps up to 4), plus 1 shower and toilet. Check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 10:00 AM.
